### General

* [Popper Selections by Karl R. Popper](https://press.princeton.edu/books/paperback/9780691020310/popper-selections)
* [The Art of Doing Science and Engineering: Learning to Learn by Richard W. Hamming](https://calhoun.nps.edu/handle/10945/62468)
* [The Mythical Man-Month by Frederick P. Brooks, Jr.](http://www.cs.unc.edu/~brooks/)
* [The Timeless Way of Building by Christopher Alexander](http://www.patternlanguage.com/bookstore/timeless-way-of-building.html)

### Foundations

* [Foundations of Data Science by Avrim Blum, John Hopcroft and Ravindran Kannan](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/jeh/book.pdf)
* [Mathematics Foundations of Data Science by Gabriel Peyré](https://mathematical-tours.github.io/book/)
* [Algebra, Topology, Differential Calculus and Optimization Theory for Computer Science and Machine Learning by Jean Gallier and Jocelyn Quaintance](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~jean/gbooks/geomath.html)

### Calculus

* [Exploring ODEs by Lloyd N. Trefethen, Ásgeir Birkisson and Tobin A. Driscoll](https://people.maths.ox.ac.uk/trefethen/ExplODE/)

### Linear Algebra

 * [Iterative Methods for Sparse Linear Systems by Yousef Saad](https://www-users.cs.umn.edu/~saad/books.html)
 * [Numerical Linear Algebra by Lloyd N. Trefethen and David Bau](https://people.maths.ox.ac.uk/trefethen/text.html) 
 * [Numerical Methods for Large Eigenvalue Problems by Yousef Saad](https://www-users.cs.umn.edu/~saad/books.html)

### Machine Learning

* [Bayesian Reasoning and Machine Learning by David Barber](http://www.cs.ucl.ac.uk/staff/d.barber/brml/)
* [Deep Learning by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio and Aaron Courville](http://www.deeplearningbook.org/)
* [Gaussian Processes for Machine Learning by Carl Edward Rasmussen and Christopher K. I. Williams](http://www.gaussianprocess.org/gpml/)
* [Information Theory, Inference, and Learning Algorithms by David J. C. Mackay](http://www.inference.org.uk/mackay/itila/book.html)
* [Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective by Kevin Murphy](http://www.cs.ubc.ca/~murphyk/MLbook/)
* [Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning by Christopher Bishop](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/cmbishop/prml-book/)

### Optimisation

* [Convex Optimisation by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/)

### Quantitative Finance

* [Options, Futures and Other Derivatives by John Hull](https://www-2.rotman.utoronto.ca/~hull/ofod/index.html)
* [The Mathematics of Financial Derivatives by Paul Wilmott, Sam Howison and Jeff Dewynne](https://www.cambridge.org/9780521497893)
